Arellano University (AU) is a private, non-sectarian university in the Philippines. Since its establishment in 1938, the university has been committed to providing quality education at an affordable cost, staying true to its motto: "For the Youth, For the Country."

Named after Cayetano Arellano, the first Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the Philippines, the university continues his legacy of integrity and service through excellence in education.

Founded by Florentino Cayco, Sr., the first Filipino Undersecretary of Public Instruction, the institution began as the Arellano Law College. Over the decades, it has evolved into a comprehensive university system with seven campuses across Metro Manila, serving thousands of students in diverse fields of study.

Our Identity

Vision

To be a premier institution of higher learning in the country, committed to the holistic development of individuals who are globally competitive and socially responsive.

Mission

To provide quality and relevant education through a diverse range of programs, innovative teaching methods, and a culture of research and community service.

Core Values

The AU community is guided by these fundamental principles:

  • Competence: Pursuing mastery and professional excellence in all academic and vocational disciplines.
  • Character: Cultivating ethical leadership, integrity, and personal responsibility.
  • Community: Fostering a spirit of service and contribution to the Filipino nation.
